STEP 1: IDEA VALIDATION

Stop Guessing. Start Knowing.

The biggest mistake new creators make? Building something nobody wants. We're going to fix that before you waste a single hour.

The 10-Minute Research Trick
Method 1: The Pain Point Hunt
- Go to Reddit - Search for communities related to your expertise
- Look for complaint posts - Sort by "top" and look for recurring problems
- Screenshot 5-10 problems that keep showing up
- Ask yourself: "Can I solve this with my knowledge?"

Method 2: The Amazon Review Scan
- Find 3-5 books in your topic area on Amazon
- Read the 2-3 star reviews (not 1-star or 5-star)
- Look for phrases like: "I wish this covered..." or "Missing information about..."
- Those gaps are your opportunities

Method 3: The Direct Ask
- Post in relevant Facebook groups or LinkedIn:
"Quick question: What's your biggest struggle with [your topic]? Working on something that might help."
